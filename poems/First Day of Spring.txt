March-time brings with him
A communal emptiness.
The sunshine he offers
Is tinted

With shades of grey, is a
Package of expired cucumbers, is a
Crumpled check from
The government. I don’t

---
Revel in the miracle of
Birdsong. I don’t laugh at
Flowers or sprouts coming
Up from the earth because I’m busy
Blowing my nose.

I come home and worry about
The words I spent the day saying.
I worry so much I forget
The feel of my hands,
The hairs on my head,
The bottoms of my feet.

---
I get tired of dreaming and never doing,

And I dream of Summertime

Which is drinking fizzy lemonade and
Digging the bottoms of my feet
Into warm sand
With you.

So goodbye, March-time
Your entangling glances
Couldn’t keep me long

---
I’ve got a new friend June-time
He brings me daisies
On a silver platter
